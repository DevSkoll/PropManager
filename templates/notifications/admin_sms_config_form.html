Twilio Setup Guide
Getting Your Credentials
  1. Log in to your Twilio Console
  2. Your Account SID and Auth Token are on the main dashboard
  3. Your Phone Number is under Phone Numbers → Manage → Active numbers
Phone Number Format
  • Use E.164 format: +15551234567
  • Include the + prefix and country code
Trial Account Limitations
  • Trial accounts can only send SMS to verified numbers
  • Messages include a "Sent from a Twilio trial account" prefix
  • Upgrade to a paid account for production use
After saving, use the Test Connection button on the Communications Settings page to verify your Twilio credentials.
