SMTP Setup Guide
Gmail
  • Host: smtp.gmail.com
  • Port: 587 (TLS) or 465 (SSL)
  • Username: Your full Gmail address
  • Password: Use an App Password (not your account password)
Microsoft 365 / Outlook
  • Host: smtp.office365.com
  • Port: 587 (TLS)
  • Username: Your full email address
  • Password: Your account password or app password
Custom SMTP
  • Check your email provider's documentation for SMTP settings
  • Use TLS on port 587 or SSL on port 465
  • Do not enable both TLS and SSL
After saving, use the Test Connection button on the Communications Settings page to verify your configuration.
