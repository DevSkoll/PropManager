## Getting Started

### Logging In

PropManager uses passwordless authentication for security and convenience:

1. Visit your property's tenant portal
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Send Login Link**
4. Check your email for the magic link
5. Click the link to log in instantly

**Tips:**
- Check your spam folder if you don't see the email
- Links expire after 15 minutes for security
- You can request a new link at any time

### First-Time Setup

When you first log in:

1. Verify your contact information
2. Add a phone number for SMS notifications (optional)
3. Set your notification preferences
4. Add a payment method for convenient online payments

## Making Payments

### Viewing Your Balance

1. From the dashboard, view your **Current Balance**
2. Click **View Details** to see all invoices
3. Each invoice shows:
   - Invoice date
   - Due date
   - Line items (rent, fees, etc.)
   - Payment status

### Making a One-Time Payment

1. Click **Make a Payment** on dashboard
2. Enter the payment amount or select **Pay Full Balance**
3. Choose your payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Bank Account (ACH)
   - PayPal
   - Bitcoin (if available)
4. Review payment details
5. Click **Submit Payment**
6. You'll receive a confirmation email

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Account** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Choose the type:
   - **Card** - Enter card number, expiration, CVV
   - **Bank Account** - Link via Plaid or enter details
4. Optionally set as default payment method
5. Click **Save**

### Setting Up AutoPay

Never miss a payment with AutoPay:

1. Go to **Account** → **AutoPay**
2. Click **Enable AutoPay**
3. Select your default payment method
4. Choose when to pay:
   - **On due date** - Pay exactly on due date
   - **Before due date** - Specify days before
5. Click **Save Settings**

### Viewing Payment History

1. Go to **Payments** → **History**
2. View all past payments with:
   - Date
   - Amount
   - Payment method
   - Confirmation number
3. Click any payment to view receipt

### Downloading Receipts

1. Go to payment history
2. Find the payment
3. Click **Download Receipt**
4. PDF receipt will download

## Viewing Your Lease

### Lease Overview

1. Click **My Lease** in navigation
2. View your current lease details:
   - Property and unit information
   - Lease dates (start and end)
   - Monthly rent amount
   - Security deposit

### Lease Terms

View the terms and conditions of your lease:

- Pet policy
- Smoking policy
- Maximum occupants
- Parking allocation
- Subletting rules
- Insurance requirements

### Registered Occupants

See who is listed on your lease:

- Primary tenant (you)
- Additional occupants
- Relationship to primary
- Co-signers if applicable

### Registered Pets

If you have registered pets:

- Pet name and type
- Breed information
- Service animal status

### Additional Fees

View any additional monthly fees:

- Pet rent
- Parking fees
- Storage fees
- Other charges

### Signing Your Lease

If your lease requires electronic signature:

1. You'll receive an email with a signing link
2. Click the link (valid for 7 days)
3. Review the full lease document
4. Draw your signature on the canvas
5. Type your full name to confirm
6. Click **Submit Signature**
7. You'll receive a confirmation

**Note:** If your signing link expired, contact your property manager to resend it.

## Submitting Work Orders

### Creating a Work Order

1. Click **Work Orders** → **Submit Request**
2. Select the issue category:
   - Plumbing
   - Electrical
   - HVAC
   - Appliances
   - Structural
   - Other
3. Describe the problem in detail
4. Set the priority:
   - **Emergency** - Safety hazard, needs immediate attention
   - **High** - Major inconvenience, needs quick response
   - **Normal** - Standard maintenance
   - **Low** - Minor issue, can wait
5. Add photos if helpful (recommended)
6. Specify access instructions if needed
7. Click **Submit**

### What Happens Next

1. Property management receives your request
2. They'll acknowledge and schedule
3. You'll receive updates via email/SMS
4. A contractor may contact you
5. Work is completed and you're notified

### Tracking Work Orders

1. Go to **Work Orders** → **My Requests**
2. View status of all work orders:
   - **Submitted** - Awaiting review
   - **Acknowledged** - Being scheduled
   - **In Progress** - Work underway
   - **Pending Parts** - Waiting for materials
   - **Completed** - Work finished
3. Click any work order for details

### Adding Notes

To provide additional information:

1. Open the work order
2. Scroll to **Notes** section
3. Type your message
4. Click **Add Note**

### Work Order Tips

- Be specific about the problem location
- Describe when the issue started
- Note any temporary fixes you've tried
- Include your availability for access
- Upload clear photos showing the issue

## Rewards Program

PropManager may offer a tenant rewards program. Check if your property participates.

### How Rewards Work

Earn rewards by:

- **Paying on time** - Build payment streaks
- **Prepaying rent** - Get bonuses for advance payments
- **Referring tenants** - Earn referral bonuses (if offered)

### Streak Rewards

Build consecutive on-time payment streaks:

| Streak | Typical Reward |
|--------|---------------|
| 3 months | $25 credit |
| 6 months | $50 credit |
| 12 months | $100 credit |

*Actual amounts set by your property manager.*

### Prepayment Bonuses

Pay multiple months ahead to earn:

| Prepayment | Typical Bonus |
|------------|---------------|
| 2 months | $15 credit |
| 3 months | $30 credit |
| 6 months | $75 credit |

### Viewing Your Rewards

1. Go to **Rewards** in navigation
2. See:
   - Current points/credit balance
   - Current streak count
   - Reward history
   - Available rewards

### Using Rewards

Rewards are typically applied as credits toward:

- Future rent payments
- Late fees (if applicable)
- Other charges

Contact your property manager for specific reward redemption options.

## Notification Preferences

### Managing Notifications

1. Go to **Account** → **Notifications**
2. Configure preferences for each notification type:

### Notification Types

| Notification | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| Payment Reminders | Upcoming payment due dates |
| Payment Confirmations | Successful payment receipts |
| Invoice Ready | New invoice available |
| Work Order Updates | Maintenance request status changes |
| Lease Reminders | Expiration and renewal notices |
| General Announcements | Property-wide communications |
| Weather Alerts | Severe weather notifications |

### Delivery Channels

Choose how you receive each notification:

- **Email** - Delivered to your registered email
- **SMS** - Text message (requires phone number)
- **In-App** - Notification in the portal
- **All** - Receive via all channels
- **None** - Disable this notification type

### Setting Preferences

1. Find the notification type
2. Select delivery channel(s)
3. Click **Save Preferences**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Payments

**Q: When is my rent due?**
A: Check your lease or dashboard for your specific due date, typically the 1st of the month.

**Q: Can I pay with cash?**
A: Cash payments are typically not accepted online. Contact your property manager for alternatives.

**Q: What if my payment fails?**
A: You'll receive a notification. Try again with a different payment method or contact your property manager.

**Q: Can I split my rent payment?**
A: Some properties allow partial payments. Check with your property manager.

### Work Orders

**Q: What qualifies as an emergency?**
A: Safety hazards (fire, flood, gas leak), no heat in winter, no water, security issues.

**Q: How long will repairs take?**
A: Response times vary by priority. Emergencies: immediate. Normal: 1-5 business days.

**Q: Can I be present during repairs?**
A: You can request to be present. Include your availability in the work order.

### Lease

**Q: When should I renew my lease?**
A: Contact your property manager 60-90 days before expiration to discuss renewal.

**Q: Can I add a roommate?**
A: Additional occupants must be approved. Contact your property manager.

**Q: What happens if I break my lease?**
A: Review your lease terms for early termination conditions and fees.
